Augmented Solow Growth Model

During the Fall 2023 semester, I worked on a group project where we explored the Solow Growth Model and added a few variations. The Solow Growth Model is a macroeconomic model that shows a country's GDP over time based on a number of factors, such as starting capital, savings rate, and worker productivity.
Our project explored how the model would change if one accounted for wars, changes in population dynamics, technology shocks, variable worker productivity, and other factors.
